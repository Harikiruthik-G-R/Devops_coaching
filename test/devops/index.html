Kongu Engineering College

Established in 1984, KEC is a premier engineering institution known for academic excellence, research innovation, and producing industry-ready graduates. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, KEC has been shaping engineering minds for decades.

GUVI - IIT Madras Incubated

GUVI is India's leading vernacular EdTech platform, incubated by IIT Madras and IIM Ahmedabad. With over 2 million learners, GUVI specializes in tech skill development, offering courses in programming, data science, AI/ML, and full-stack development.
